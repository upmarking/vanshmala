The Idea: In Profile Setup, offer a "Timeline Mode" alongside the standard form. Ask questions like "When was your Mundan/First Job/Wedding?" and plot them on a visual line.
Integration: Sync this with your existing Timeline feature.

5. "Kinsman" Relationship Context
On Instagram, you see "Followed by..." to understand context. On VanshMala, context is kinship.

The Idea: When User A views User B's profile, dynamically calculate and display the relationship text at the very top.
Example: Instead of just "Bio", show: "Amit is your Paternal First Cousin (Chacha's Son)."
How: Use the underlying graph data to find the shortest path between the logged-in user and the profile being viewed. This immediately grounds the user in their relationship.
